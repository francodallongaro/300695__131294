Who am I

My name is Cami Mostaza and I'm passionate about designing creative, meaningful and impactful experience to improve people’s life.

I'm constantly looking to challenge myself to learn new skills and work with new people in multi-disciplinary teams.
I am a curious and eager-learner by nature.

Interested in designing innovative, learning & nature-related projects. I’m a communicator, a storyteller and a collaborator.

I am very much a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to development.
Currently, specializing in Ux research and interaction design.

My journey

Im an Architect with an honor-degree in Buenos Aires University.
I have performed as a design and technical teacher there as well.

Moreover, Im an Expert at Excellence in design for greater efficiencies (EDGE). Im very interested in green-design and climate change-related projects.

I also perform as a singer and a paintor, unleashing my interior artist.

All this background immensely enriches me in my day-to-day designer’s projects. Outside of work you’ll usually find me taking long treks, singing or chasing my dog down the park. Beware, a life-philosophy conversation with me could last hours. That’s just me.
